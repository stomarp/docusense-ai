# DocuGuard

DocuGuard is a backend-focused AI/ML system designed to analyze HR policy documents
and help identify compliance gaps and unclear or risky language.

## Why DocuGuard?
HR policy documents such as employee handbooks and codes of conduct are long,
frequently updated, and manually reviewed. This makes it easy to miss required
sections or use ambiguous wording that can lead to confusion or risk.

DocuGuard assists by automatically analyzing these documents and highlighting
potential issues for human review.

## What DocuGuard Does
- Accepts HR policy documents (PDF or text)
- Extracts and processes document text
- Detects missing mandatory policy sections
- Flags ambiguous or risky language
- Generates a structured and explainable compliance report
